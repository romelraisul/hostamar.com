Every reply leads with the pain they already pay, then the 34% saving. Never
defend the price in isolation.

den 3 ta tool e. Hostamar e **৳3500** e hosting + 100 video + chat + browser sob,
**34% kom**. ROI calculator e dekhun: 30 reel × ৳500 agency = ৳15000 — apni save **৳11500/mo**."

"CapCut e watermark + English voice + 1 ghonta edit. Hostamar 30s e **Bangla voice**
+ apnar **bKash checkout link** auto. Time = taka — 30 reel e 30 ghonta bachchen."

## "Try kore dekhi"  (let me think)
"Perfect — **5 ta free**, kono bKash lagbe na. 3 ta product er image den,
ami 3 ta baniye dei ekhuni. Apni post koren, response dekhen. Valo lagle upgrade."

## "Amader already agency ache"  (we have an editor)
"Agency ৳500/reel = 30 reel e ৳15000. Hostamar e ৳3500 — **৳11500 bachchen**,
ar video 30s e ready, agency er 3 din wait nay. Ekta reel free banai, compare koren."

## "bKash e payment secure?"  (is payment safe)
"bKash tokenized checkout — apnar PIN bKash app e, amra dekhina. Payment
`status=paid` holei account active. Trust packet + /status public — audit korte paren."

"12 product, 2 reel — exactly apnar jonno. 5 free video te 2 ta extra reel banan,
boost koren, sales baran. Boro howar agei system ta ready thakuk."
