# Getting Started with Hostamar Cloud Hosting: Your Complete Onboarding Guide

Whether you're launching a business website, deploying a web application, or setting up AI-powered tools, Hostamar Cloud Hosting provides everything you need under one roof. This guide walks you through the first steps — from account creation to your first deployment.

Hostamar isn't just a hosting provider. It's an all-in-one platform that combines:

- **Cloud hosting** — Deploy websites and applications on optimized infrastructure
- **AI video generation** — Create marketing videos without expensive equipment
- **AI Chat (DuckAI)** — Your intelligent assistant for business and creativity
- **AI Browser** — Privacy-focused browsing with AI capabilities
- **Dev IDE** — Code, build, and deploy directly from your browser
- **LuckyStar Gaming** — Lightweight entertainment options

Everything is integrated, so your hosting account becomes the hub for your entire digital presence.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [Hostamar.com](https://hostamar.com)

## Step 3: Set Up Your First Project

### Option A: Deploy a Pre-Built Template

Hostamar offers one-click deployment of popular templates:

- Business website
- Landing page
- Portfolio
- E-commerce storefront
- Blog

Select your template, customize colors and content, and deploy in under 5 minutes.

### Option B: Upload Your Own Site

If you have existing files:

1. Go to your **Dashboard** → **Projects**
2. Click **New Project** → **Upload Files**
3. Drag and drop your HTML, CSS, and JS files
4. Configure your domain (or use the free *.hostamar.app subdomain)
5. Click **Deploy**

### Option C: Use the Dev IDE

For developers:

1. Navigate to **Dev IDE** from your dashboard
2. Start coding with our browser-based development environment
3. Use the built-in terminal, file browser, and preview
4. Deploy directly with one click — no FTP or CLI needed

The Dev IDE comes pre-configured with Node.js, Python, and Git support.

## Step 4: Configure Your Domain

- Use the free subdomain `your-project.hostamar.app` to start immediately
- Connect a custom domain via your dashboard — we support DNS management
- Free SSL certificates via Cloudflare integration included on all plans
